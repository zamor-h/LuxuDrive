Our Story

LuxuDrive was born from a simple yet powerful idea: car rental should be as enjoyable as the journey itself. Founded by automotive enthusiasts Sarah Chen and Michael Rodriguez in 2018, we recognized that traditional rental companies often fell short in delivering the seamless, transparent experience customers deserved.

What started as a small operation in Miami with just 25 vehicles has grown into a global network spanning over 50 locations across North America, Europe, and Asia. Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition as one of the industry's most trusted and innovative rental companies.

Today, we proudly serve over 500,000 customers annually, offering a meticulously maintained fleet of more than 2,000 vehicles ranging from economical city cars to luxury sedans and high-performance sports cars. Every vehicle in our fleet undergoes rigorous maintenance and safety inspections to ensure your journey is not just comfortable, but truly exceptional.
